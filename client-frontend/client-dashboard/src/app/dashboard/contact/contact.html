Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a freelancer?

Simply sign up for an account, complete your profile with your skills and portfolio, and start browsing available projects. You can also set up your availability and rates.

How do I post a project as a client?

Create a client account, go to the Projects section, and click "Create New Project". Fill in the project details, budget, and timeline, then publish it for freelancers to see.

What are the platform fees?

We charge a small service fee of 3% on successful projects. This helps us maintain the platform and provide customer support.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. Clients can pay via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer. Freelancers receive payments after project completion and client approval.
